About

A project to analyse Bach Chorales. Currently, it can check the presence of parallel 5ths and 8ths. Future implementations of possibly: detection of cadences, labelling of chords, generation of bach chorales. How it works: At every instance when there are 4 'on' note events, a 'chord' is formed. These chords will then be used to check for parallels between them. When uploading the midi file, ensure that is has strictly only 4 parts.
